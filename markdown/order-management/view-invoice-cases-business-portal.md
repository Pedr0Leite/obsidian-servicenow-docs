# View invoice case details in the Business Portal

View invoice case details, status, activity history, and attachments from the Business Portal.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_customerservice.customer

## Procedure

1.  Log in to the Business Portal.

2.  Select **Requests** &gt; **View submitted requests** &gt; **View cases &amp; tasks**.

3.  Select **My invoice cases** from the Categories menu to view invoice cases.

4.  Select a case to view its state and other details.

    For a description of the Invoice case lines list view, see [[invoice-case-header-list-view|Invoice case details on the Business Portal]].

    For more information about invoice states, see [[invoice-case-states|Invoice case states on Business Portal]].
