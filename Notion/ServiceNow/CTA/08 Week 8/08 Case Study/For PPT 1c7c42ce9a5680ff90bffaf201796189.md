# For PPT

### **Week 8: Service Portal, AI & Generative AI, Virtual Agent & Chat**

### **Key Factors & Challenges**

- **Multiple Customer Support Channels** → Current setup is fragmented across web, phone, email, and self-service portals.
- **No Gen AI Experience** → Implementation is a **pilot** for broader enterprise-wide AI adoption.
- **Key Objectives** → Reduce MTTR, increase deflection rates, improve agent and developer productivity, and enhance infrastructure alert analysis.

---

### **Discussion Points & Solutions**

✅ **Challenges with a New Virtual Agent**

- **User Adoption & Change Resistance** → Ensure intuitive UI, proactive training, and feedback loops.
- **Accuracy & NLP Training** → Leverage ServiceNow AI models and fine-tune with Sovereign Bank’s historical data.
- **Integration with Existing Workflows** → Use **Conversational Interfaces** and **AI-powered routing** to seamlessly integrate with ITSM, HR, and CSM.

✅ **Gen AI Features Across HR, CSM, & ITSM**

- **HR** → Automated case resolution, knowledge article summarization, intelligent employee inquiries.
- **CSM** → AI-assisted ticket triage, predictive issue resolution, chatbot-based customer self-service.
- **ITSM** → Automated incident analysis, smart ticket clustering, and AI-driven playbook recommendations.

✅ **ServiceNow Gen AI for Infrastructure Alerts**

- **Predictive Alerting** → AI detects anomalies in logs and monitoring data.
- **Automated Root Cause Analysis** → Identifies recurring patterns in incidents.
- **Incident Prioritization & Remediation** → AI-powered classification and resolution recommendations.

✅ **Key Business Goals for Gen AI Implementation**

1. **Enhance Self-Service Efficiency** → Increase chatbot and knowledge base utilization.
2. **Boost Agent & Developer Productivity** → Automate manual ticket handling and reduce case resolution time.
3. **Optimize Infrastructure Operations** → AI-powered alert analysis and proactive incident detection.
4. **Improve User Engagement** → Personalized interactions via Virtual Agent & AI recommendations.

---

### **Presentation Recommendations**

🎯 **1. Service Portal Strategy & Impact**

- **Omnichannel Consolidation** → Unify **web, chat, email, and phone** into a single AI-powered support experience.
- **Smart Self-Service** → Enable **knowledge base automation, AI-driven recommendations, and chatbot-first interactions** to boost deflection rates.
- **Minimal Custom Development** → Use **ServiceNow's out-of-the-box Virtual Agent** and **Next Experience UI** for scalability.

🎯 **2. Gen AI MVP Implementation Across IT, HR, & CSM**

- **HR** → Virtual assistant for HR inquiries, automated case summaries.
- **CSM** → Smart case routing, AI-powered customer sentiment analysis.
- **ITSM** → Predictive ticket management, automated diagnostics & remediation suggestions.

🎯 **3. Gen AI Release Roadmap**

**Phase 1** (0-3 months) → AI-powered **knowledge search, Virtual Agent FAQs, and initial chatbot automation**.

**Phase 2** (3-6 months) → Expand Virtual Agent to handle **ticket classification, case routing, and proactive alert analysis**.

**Phase 3** (6-12 months) → Implement **Gen AI-driven insights, predictive analytics, and self-healing infrastructure automation**.

🎯 **4. Initial Metrics to Measure Virtual Agent & Gen AI Success**

- **Deflection Rate** → % of issues resolved via AI without human intervention.
- **MTTR Reduction** → Time saved in resolving IT and HR cases.
- **Agent & Developer Productivity** → # of AI-suggested actions vs. manually handled tasks.
- **User Adoption** → % of employees/customers interacting with AI-powered self-service tools.

By implementing these strategies, **Sovereign Bank will simplify its customer service, improve efficiency, and lay the foundation for enterprise-wide AI adoption.** 🚀
