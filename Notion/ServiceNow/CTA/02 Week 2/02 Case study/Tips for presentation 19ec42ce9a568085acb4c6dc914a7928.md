# Tips for presentation

### **Approach for Your 10-Minute Presentation on CMDB & CSDM at Sovereign Bank**

This presentation must **convince resistant stakeholders** (Li Na & the Configuration Manager) of the **value of CMDB and CSDM**, address **business gaps**, and provide a **clear roadmap** for implementation.

---

### **1. Start with the Problem Statement (1-2 Minutes)**

- Sovereign Bank's **Remedy system is highly customized**, making **CMDB governance difficult**.
- Data is **inconsistent and scattered** across different tools with no unified model.
- Resistance to change exists due to **long-standing processes and fragmented ownership**.
- **Business impact**: Inefficiencies, difficulty in service ownership identification, and compliance risks.

---

### **2. Critical Gaps & Challenges (2-3 Minutes)**

**IT & Business Gaps**

- **Lack of a centralized best-in-class CMDB** → Impacts visibility, governance, and automation.
- **No standard data model** → Leads to **misaligned processes across BUs** and slows decision-making.
- **Limited HR instance adoption** → HR data is underutilized, impacting user onboarding & governance.
- **Inconsistent discovery sources** → Data coming from **multiple third-party tools lacks integrity**.

**Key Challenges in Implementation**

- **Resistance from ITSM teams** (process owners, helpdesk staff) due to past reliance on Remedy.
- **Li Na’s push for heavy customizations** conflicts with the goal of **"fit the platform" best practices**.
- **Leadership needs clarity on roles and responsibilities** to implement CMDB & ITOM successfully.

---

### **3. Solution: Why CMDB & CSDM? (3 Minutes)**

- **CSDM standardizes data across ITSM, ITOM, and HR** → Provides a **single source of truth**.
- **Aligns application and service portfolios** → Enables clear **service ownership in Change Management**.
- **Automated discovery & data certification** → Reduces technical debt and **ensures CMDB accuracy**.
- **Supports scalability across Sovereign Bank Group** → Future-proof and integrates with ITOM.

**Quick Wins to Gain Stakeholder Buy-In**

- **Automate discovery for quick CMDB health improvements** (ServiceNow Discovery replacing third-party tools).
- **Identify service owners & map to Change Management approvals** → Immediate impact on operations.
- **Leverage HR instance for role-based access management** → Enhances governance.

---

### **4. Implementation Roadmap (2 Minutes)**

1. **Crawl** – Establish **CMDB baseline** (data cleansing, CSDM alignment, stakeholder engagement).
2. **Walk** – **Automate discovery & enforce governance** (role-based ownership, change approvals).
3. **Run** – Integrate **ITOM, service mapping, and compliance monitoring**.
4. **Fly** – **Predictive insights, AI-driven automation, and full ITSM-ITOM maturity**.

---

### **5. Closing & Next Steps (1 Minute)**

- **Assign roles for CMDB governance** → Define service owners, change approvers, and platform owners.
- **Leadership engagement** → Define the input needed for CMDB & ITOM success.
- **Plan a phased rollout** → Ensure adoption before expanding across Sovereign Bank Group.
