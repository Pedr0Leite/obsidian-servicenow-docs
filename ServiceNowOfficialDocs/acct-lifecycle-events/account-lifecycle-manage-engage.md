# Manage engagements

Create and manage customer [[account-lifecycle-engagement|engagements]], success blueprints, [[account-lifecycle-touchpoints|touchpoints]], risk signals, and success plays throughout the [[account-lifecycle-events-customer-success-about|customer success]] journey.

An engagement centralizes the activities involved in a customer's success journey. Customer success managers use [[account-lifecycle-manage-engagement|engagements]] to define success criteria with blueprints, track health and risk, run success plays, and manage [[account-lifecycle-use-touchpoints|touchpoints]] with customers throughout the engagement lifecycle.

-   **[[account-lifecycle-view-engage|Engagement home page]]**  
This page provides detailed information of an engagement including internal and external stakeholders, upcoming touchpoints, initiatives, and blueprints.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-engage|Create an engagement]]**  
Create an engagement record that centralizes all internal and external activities that are involved in the customer success journey.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-touchpoint|Create a touchpoint]]**  
Create a touchpoint to capture and support conversations between providers and customers during the engagement lifecycle.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-risk-signal|Create a risk signal]]**  
Create a risk signal to identify any risk or issues that can occur during the engagement lifecycle.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-success-play|Create a success play]]**  
Use this option to create a success play to create automated actions.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-success-blueprint|Create a success blueprint]]**  
Create a [[account-lifecycle-success-blueprint|success blueprint]] to define the success objectives, primary success outcomes, and success initiatives for an engagement.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-applicable-outcome|Create an applicable success outcome]]**  
Create the applicable success outcomes to support the primary success outcome.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-cancel-success-initiative|Close or cancel a success initiative]]**  
You can close or cancel a success initiative and all the related tasks.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-view-success-object|Success objective record page]]**  
This page provides a detailed view of the success objective and its related outcomes and initiatives as defined in the success blueprint.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-view-sucess-outcome|Primary success outcome record page]]**  
A primary success outcome refers to the result or goal that is used to measure the achievement of a success objective.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-success-score-calc|Measure success rate]]**  
Measure and track the effectiveness and completion of success objectives and outcomes within a project or engagement with success scores and success rates.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-success-roadmap|Success initiative roadmap]]**  
The success initiative roadmap displays success initiatives and their outcomes over time in a visual timeline. Use it to track initiative progress, filter by status, and adjust the view by timescale or grouping.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-imp-record|Create an implementation record]]**  
Track the progress of a partner or customer implementing a product or a service.
