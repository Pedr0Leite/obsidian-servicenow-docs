# Manage customer success playbooks

Use the [[account-lifecycle-cust-succ-playbook|customer success playbooks]] to manage planned and unplanned activities during an engagement. Configure the structure of these playbooks, including their stages and activities, use the Playbook builder.

-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-internal-play-playbook|Create an internal play]]**  
Create an [[account-lifecycle-internal-playbook|internal play]] to define planned or unplanned activities that the customer does not have access to during the engagement lifecycle.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-success-case-playbook|Create a customer play]]**  
Create a [[account-lifecycle-success-playbook|customer play]] in collaboration with customers to define planned and unplanned activities required to support an engagement.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-renewal-playbook|Create a renewal play]]**  
Create a renewal playbook to define processes to simplify contract renewals and identify expansion opportunities.
