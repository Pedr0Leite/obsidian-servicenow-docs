# Plan your installation

Identify the requirements and [[plan-installation-ms-teams|plan your installation]] of the [[hiring-experiences-landing|Hiring Experiences]] suite.

**Parent Topic:**[Configuring Hiring Experiences](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery/config-frmwrk-ta.md)

## Dependencies of Hiring Experiences

The following plugins are required to use all the features in the Hiring Experiences suite.

Hiring Experiences has the following major components:

-   [[hiring-dashboard|Hiring tab]] \(sn\_ta\_hiring\_mgr\)
-   [[recruitment-workspace-concept|Recruitment workspace]] \(sn\_ta\_recruiter\)
-   [[applicant-center-concept|Applicant Center]] \(sn\_ta\_applicant\)
-   [[hiring-tab-concept|Hiring]] Connector \(sn\_ta\_connector\)
-   [[talent-profile-landing|Talent Profile]] \(sn\_ta\_tp\)
-   [[interview-management-landing|Interview Management]] \(sn\_ta\_int\_mgmt\)

When you install all the plugins within the Hiring Experiences suite, the following plugins are installed by default.

-   [[employee-center-pro-landing|Employee Center Pro]] \(sn\_ex\_sp\_pro\)
-   [[manager-hub-overview|Manager Hub]] \(sn\_mh\)
-   [[emp-slate-employee-profile|Employee Profile]] \(com.sn\_employee\)
-   Content Experiences \(sn\_ca\)
-   [[skills-intelligence|Skills Foundation]] \(sn\_skills\_int\)
-   Finance Common Architecture \(sn\_fin\)
-   [[hiring-core|Hiring Core]] \(sn\_ta\_hiring\_core\)
-   Time Limited Authentication \(com.snc.authenticate.time\_limited\_authentication\)
-   Explicit roles \(com.glide.explicit\_roles\)
