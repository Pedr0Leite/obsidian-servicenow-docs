# Exploring Self-service

The [[self-service-landing|Self-Service]] application gives supported end users a clean, simple front end to their IT support organization.

By default, the Self-Service application provides access to common actions, such as viewing your homepage, accessing the [[service-catalog|Service Catalog]], viewing knowledge articles, working with incidents, and taking [[r_SurveyManagementLandingPage|surveys]].

-   **[Service catalog requests](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/servicenow-platform/self-service/p_ServiceCatalogRequests.md)**  
This page contains links to content on [[p_ServiceCatalogRequests|service catalog requests]].
-   **[Service Delegation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/servicenow-platform/self-service/c_ServiceDelegation.md)**  
[[c_ServiceDelegation|Service delegation]] is the ability to designate other users to view and interact with approvals sent or tasks assigned to you, and to receive copies of all [[email|email]] notifications sent to you.
