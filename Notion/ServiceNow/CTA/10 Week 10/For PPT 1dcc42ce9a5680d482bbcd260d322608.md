# For PPT

**Slide 1: Title SlideTitle:** Week 10 – Process Automation Design (PAD), Intelligent Workflows, and Citizen Developer Governance

**Subtitle:** Sovereign Bank Case Study – Certified Technical Architect Program

**Presented to:** Li Na (Enterprise Architect) & Albert Wan (Platform Owner)

---

**Slide 2: Recap & Objective**

**Last Week:** We mapped ServiceNow into Sovereign Bank's enterprise architecture, prioritized key integrations, and defined foundational data strategies.

**This Week's Objective:**

- Present a Process Automation Design (PAD) use case.
- Outline a governance model for a Citizen Developer program.
- Recommend a high-value application for citizen development.
- Define analytics from the start to improve operational intelligence.

These initiatives will drive cross-enterprise process unification and reduce IT workload.

**Slide 3: Key Business Outcomes**

- **Unified Cross-Enterprise Workflows** → Provide a single process view across business units.
- **Governance for Citizen Developers** → Define roles, responsibilities, and training plans.
- **Empowerment of Staff** → Reduce IT burden and increase business agility.
- **Intelligent Process Monitoring** → Link KPIs to goals and continuously improve.

These outcomes align with Sovereign Bank’s goals for process automation, operational clarity, and scalability.

---

**Slide 4: Why PAD? Use Case for Incident ManagementProblem:**

- Different divisions have unique incident processes due to past mergers.
- Manual assignments and approvals add delays and inconsistency.

**PAD Use Case:**

- **Process Name:** Unified Incident Lifecycle
- **Steps:**
    1. Auto-categorize incidents based on affected CI and service tier.
    2. Trigger adaptive approvals based on urgency and impacted users.
    3. Use flow branches to handle regional variations (e.g., EMEA vs. NA).
    4. Send real-time updates to Slack/MS Teams.

**Roles Involved:**

- **Incident Coordinator (Bank)**
- **ITSM Process Owner (Bank)**
- **PAD Designer (ServiceNow or Consultant)**

PAD gives Sovereign a flexible visual interface for collaboration, logic building, and optimization without writing custom code.

---

**Slide 5: Challenges in Adopting PAD**

- **Script-Heavy History:** Legacy logic must be reimagined into flows.
- **Poor CMDB Data:** Affects condition logic and approvals.
- **Lack of Workflow Maturity:** Teams may struggle to understand dependencies.

**Mitigation Strategy:**

- Run **co-design workshops** with ServiceNow architects.
- Introduce **guided flow development** with guardrails.
- Use **test data sets** to simulate scenarios and validate outcomes early.

---

**Slide 6: PAD Architecture Considerations**
Key Principles for Building PAD Use Cases:

- Keep **logic modular** and **reusable**.
- Align to **existing data structures and integration points**.
- Include **decision branches for region-specific flows**.
- Always integrate with **Notifications**, **Flow Designer**, and **Performance Analytics**.

This ensures process logic remains scalable and maintainable.

---

**Slide 7: Citizen Developer Governance Model**
Using the **3 Pillars: Enable, Equip, Control**

**Enable:**

- **Roles:** Business Analyst, Process Owner, Citizen Developer
- **Skillsets:** Basic ServiceNow UI building, Flow Designer familiarity, understanding of business workflows
- **Training:** Use Now Learning paths + internal coaching

**Equip:**

- **Data Dependencies:** Respect integration logic from HR (Workday, SuccessFactors) & IT (CMDB)
- **Tools:** Pre-approved spokes, App Engine Studio templates
- **Best Practices:** Follow IntegrationHub policies and reusable components

**Control:**

- **Environment Hygiene:** Naming standards, update set policies
- **Security & Data Integrity:** Role-based access control, audit trails
- **Governance Layer:** Periodic review board, peer review before deployment

---

**Slide 8: High-Value Citizen Dev App IdeaApp:** Access & Equipment Request Portal
**Why it’s Valuable:**

- Reduces emails/spreadsheets used for onboarding/offboarding.
- Integrates HR, Facilities, IT, and Security workflows.
- Common need across all business units

**Citizen Dev Role:**

- Build the intake form, integrate HR data, and trigger multi-team fulfillment workflows using PAD and Flow Designer.

This is a perfect fit for a business-led build with centralized governance.

---

**Slide 9: Leveraging Analytics to Optimize Workflows**
Tools to Monitor and Improve:

- **Performance Analytics** → Trend-based KPI analysis from day one
- **Process Optimization** → Visualize bottlenecks and automation opportunities
- **Decision Builder** → Recommend next actions based on data trends
- **Center of Excellence Dashboards** → Monitor app usage, issue rates, developer participation

Tracking usage, ROI, and workflow gaps ensures the program grows strategically.

---

**Slide 10: Conclusion & Next Steps**

- Implement PAD for incident management as a pilot
- Launch citizen developer governance using Enable, Equip, Control
- Prioritize access request app for citizen developers
- Embed analytics early to track, learn, and optimize

With these steps, Sovereign Bank will improve process consistency, reduce IT burden, and foster innovation through governed self-service development.
