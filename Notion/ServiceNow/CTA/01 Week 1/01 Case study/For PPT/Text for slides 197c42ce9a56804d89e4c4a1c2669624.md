### Slide 6 - Data security and privacy

(Introductory topic text)

To safeguard Personally Identifiable Information such as SSNs, financial transactions, and HR data, Sovereign Bank must navigate cross-border data sharing restrictions imposed by GDPR and PDPA while mitigating cybersecurity risks across its on-premise, hybrid, and cloud environments.

(Bullet points on the slide)

ServiceNow strengthens security by enforcing Role-Based Access Controls, ensuring only authorized personnel access sensitive information. Field-Level Encryption protects critical data while maintaining usability, and Multi-Factor Authentication with Single Sign-On enhances secure access across systems. These capabilities help Sovereign Bank mitigate risks and maintain compliance.
