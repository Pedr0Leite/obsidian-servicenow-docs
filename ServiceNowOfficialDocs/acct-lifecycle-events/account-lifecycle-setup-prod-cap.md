# Configure product capabilities

Configure product capabilities, adoption roadmap templates, and capability usage tracking to measure how customers use your products.

A product capability is the higher-level ability of a product to solve a problem or deliver value. By defining capabilities and tracking their usage, [[account-lifecycle-events-customer-success-about|customer success]] teams can monitor adoption, identify expansion opportunities, and demonstrate the value customers receive from your products.

-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-prod-cap|Create a product capability record]]**  
Create a product capability record and associate with one or more capability usage records.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-par-roadmap-temp|Define a product adoption roadmap template]]**  
Create a template that defines a pre-configured adoption strategy with products or capabilities organized into lanes, that can be customized as needed.
