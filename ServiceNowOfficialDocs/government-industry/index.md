# Australia Public Sector

- [[bun-public-sector-landing-page|Public Sector Digital Services (PSDS)]] -- Use the ServiceNowPublic Sector Digital Services (PSDS) application to deliver government services to constituents, businesses, and agencies on a single platform. Built on a government data model, Public Sector Digital Services provides prebuilt playbooks, solutions, configurable portals, and an AI-powered workspace that government agents can use to manage the full lifecycle of service requests—from intake to resolution. You can deploy Public Sector Digital Services with the applications that match your agency's needs. The applications are configurable and extensible using low-code tools to build workflows that are specific to your processes. Whether you're setting up for the first time or expanding an existing implementation, the content in this documentation set guides you through exploring the solution, configuring your environment, and using the product day to day.
  - [[exploring-public-sector-digital-services|Explore]] -- Learn about Public Sector Digital Services (PSDS) features to help create a seamless support experience for constituents, businesses, internal agencies, and external agencies.
    - [[psds-explore-data-management|Data management]] -- Data management refers to the processes and tools used to collect, organize, secure, and use constituent-related data effectively so that government service agent teams can deliver better service experiences. You can give your managers and agents a comprehensive view of constituent data, including constituent details, products and services, assets, service contracts, and entitlements.
    - [[using-public-sector-workspace-for-government-agents|Playbooks and Solutions]] -- Public Sector Digital Services provides agents across government agencies with a tailored workspace experience to work on cases created by constituents, businesses, and agencies.
      - [[psds-explore-inv-case-management|Investigative Case Management]] -- The Public Sector Investigative Case Management (ICM) solutions workspace provides a single location for investigators to organize assets related to an investigation. Investigators and supervisors can use ICM to initiate and manage investigative cases, view the full context of an investigation, and get relevant recommendations.
      - [[psds-explore-grants-management|Grants Management]] -- The Grants Management solution provides government agencies with a workflow for defining grant programs, screening applications, evaluating proposals, and communicating funding decisions. The solution runs entirely on the ServiceNow platform.
        - [[psds-gm-rolling-grant-approvals-concept|Rolling grant approvals]] -- Rolling grant approvals give grant program managers the flexibility to propose and submit funding decisions for any scored subset of applications during the Funding Allocation stage. Decisions can be submitted at any time, without waiting for the entire proposal portfolio to complete review.
      - [[playbooks-psds-exploring|Playbooks]] -- A playbook provides government service agents with step-by-step guidance through the life cycle of a public service request case. Use Playbooks to fulfill requests for licenses and permits, government records and other public information, or non-emergency service requests.
        - [[psds-exploring-pbs-social-benefits|Social Benefits Playbook]] -- The Social Benefits Playbook provides an end-to-end workflow for handling applications for social benefit programs offered by your government agency and submitted by public sector end users.
        - [[psds-exploring-pbs-license-permit|License and Permit Playbook]] -- The License and Permit Playbook provides an end-to-end workflow for handling requests for the license and permit that your agency offers, submitted by public sector end users.
        - [[psds-exploring-pbs-information-request|Information Request Playbook]] -- The Information Request Playbook provides an end-to-end workflow for handling public record and information requests submitted by public sector end users under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA).
        - [[psds-exploring-pbs-service-request|Service Request Playbook]] -- The Service Request Playbook provides an end-to-end workflow for handling non-emergency service requests submitted by public sector end users.
    - [[portals-psds-exploring|Portals]] -- A portal provides an end user, in this case a constituent or applicant, with step-by-step guidance through submitting a public service request case. Use Portals to submit requests for licenses and permits, government records and public information, non-emergency services, or to submit applications for social benefits or grants opportunities.
      - [[gsp-overview-public-sector|Government Service Portal]] -- By using the Government Service Portal, constituents and businesses can report issues, access and view the status of existing service requests, request public records, search for information about a question or issue, view and update their contact information, and request assistance from a government service agent— all in a user-friendly, customizable interface.
      - [[psds-rsp-overview|Reviewer Service Portal]] -- Enable merit reviewers to track, score, and monitor grant proposals via a dedicated Reviewer Service portal.
      - [[psds-gmp-overview|Grants Management Portal]] -- Constituents and business contacts can find, apply for, and track the status of their grant applications using the Grants Management Portal.
    - [[psds-explore-case-management|Case management]] -- Manage constituent requests, applications, and issues by creating cases, routing cases to agents and groups, and working toward resolution. With case management, you can provide your government service agents with the tools they need to evaluate cases, communicate with constituents, answer questions, and resolve issues.
      - [[servicebridge-psds-exploring|Service Bridge]] -- Connect multiple ServiceNow instances to provide support and service experiences across the Public Sector​ ecosystem, enabling coordination between multiple government service agencies and their constituents using Service Bridge for Public Sector Digital Services (PSDS).
      - [[psds-policy-as-code-engine-pace|Policy as Code Engine (PaCE)]] -- As a Public Sector Digital Services process owner or admin, you can create and maintain the rules that drive how eligibility and benefits are determined in the Social Benefits Playbook using Policy as Code Engine (PaCE. PaCE is a powerful, low-code rules engine capable of processing rules in seconds. The rule-sets created within this rules engine are called policies.
    - [[psds-explore-analytics-and-insights|Analytics and insights]] -- Use the Performance Analytics applications for Public Sector Digital Services to report your constituent service performance and effectiveness. You can use the predefined key performance indicators (KPIs) and preconfigured role-based dashboards for monitoring your service processes. These analytics enable you to align your organizational goals across all roles, from executive to manager to agent.
