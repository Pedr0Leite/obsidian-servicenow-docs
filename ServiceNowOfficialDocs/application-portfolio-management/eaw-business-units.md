# Exploring business units

Business units are part of your organization that are responsible for certain operations and have their own objectives.

Business units usually comprise departments and they have their own set of requirements regarding hardware and software, to meet their organizational objectives.

Using the [[ea-workspace|Enterprise Architecture Workspace]], business units can manage their business applications effectively.
