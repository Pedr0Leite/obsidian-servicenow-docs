# Introducing Sovereign Bank

[Sovereign Bank Case Study.pdf](Introducing%20Sovereign%20Bank/Sovereign_Bank_Case_Study.pdf)

80k Employees

Main hubs in Asia, Europe, Americas

Has ServiceNow for 6 months

Has legacy software's and infrastructures

They want to be OOTB as much as possible, but they might need heavy customizations due old processes and UX/UI

Users are resistant to new tools

They used to work with waterfall but now we will work with agile style

Everything needs to align with the security requirements

They have issues with encryption

Social security numbers being shown in Cases

They need to match global regulations, that are different for country to country

They want to migrate articles to knowledge base, they have it on flat file and webservice posh(?) 

Goals:

- A centralized best in-class CMDB
- A simplified omnichannel customer support desk
- Simplification and automation of processes across the enterprise
- Increased automation of controls to reduce risk
- Compliance with country specific financial industry regulations
- Ease of upgrade (ServiceNow release +8 weeks)
- Extendibility of the platform across the Sovereign Bank Group
