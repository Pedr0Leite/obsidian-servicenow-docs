# Dashboards

Monitor engagement health, portfolio performance, and risk trends using the [[account-lifecycle-events-customer-success-about|customer success]] dashboards.

Customer success dashboards provide visibility into your engagement portfolio. Use the available dashboards to track key metrics, identify accounts that need attention, and analyze trends across your customer base.

-   **[[account-lifecycle-success-report-overview|Success report dashboard overview]]**  
The [[account-lifecycle-success-report|success report dashboard]] enables customer success managers to oversee and manage the overall customer portfolio.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-success-landing|Success portfolio overview]]**  
Get an overall view of your success portfolio including your [[account-lifecycle-engagement|engagements]], account status, escalations, and risks.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-risk-portfolio|Risk portfolio dashboard]]**  
Get an overall view of all active risks, top risk categories, and the risk scope of all [[account-lifecycle-manage-engagement|engagements]] with which you’re associated.
