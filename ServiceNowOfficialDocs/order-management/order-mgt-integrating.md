# Extending Order Management with ServiceNow applications and integrations

You can use various ServiceNow store applications and integrations to further extend the capabilities of [[explore-order-management|Order Management]].

|Application or integration|Description|
|--------------------------|-----------|
|[[service-bridge-om-for-providers|Order Management for providers with Service Exchange]]|Enable service providers and their customers to use Order Management to place and fulfill complex orders, including certain types of MACD orders, over Service Exchange.|
|[[order-mgt-integrating-fsm|Order Management integration with Field Service Management]]|Create field service work order requests automatically with Order Management, and track them in Field Service Management.|
|[[order-mgt-integrating-spm|Order Management integration with Service Portfolio Management]]|Provide project oversight of complex, long-running orders and order tasks in Order Management, and manage them in Service Portfolio Management.|
