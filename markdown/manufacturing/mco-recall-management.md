# Recall management

Recall management enables the OEMs to identify and remove or replace the faulty products sold to the customers.

|Tasks|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|[[mco-rc-my-campaigns|Create a campaign]]|Displays all the [[mco-rcl-clms|recall campaign]] that are assigned to the person logged in to the workspace.|
|[[mco-rc-all-campaigns|View All campaigns]]|Displays all the recall campaign that are created by the OEM.|
|[[mco-rc-my-campaign-phases|Create My campaign phases]]|Displays all the recall campaign phases that are assigned to the person logged in to the workspace.|
||Displays all the recall campaign phases that are created by the OEM.|

-   **[Create a campaign](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/manufacturing/mco-rc-my-campaigns.md)**  
Create a recall campaign and also view the list of campaigns claims assigned to the person who has logged in to the workspace.
-   **[View All campaigns](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/manufacturing/mco-rc-all-campaigns.md)**  
View a recall campaign using the All campaigns form.
-   **[Create My campaign phases](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/manufacturing/mco-rc-my-campaign-phases.md)**  
Display the claims assigned to the person who has logged in to the workspace.
-   **[[mco_merge_phases|Merge two or more phases]]**  
Merge phases and sub-phases to reorganize and transfer assets.
