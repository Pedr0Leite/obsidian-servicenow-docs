# Sending notifications with Everbridge

You can use Everbridge in the [[bcm-workspace|BCM Configurable Workspace]] to send emergency notifications and inform stakeholders about [[crisis-management-uib|crisis events]].

BCM program manager sends out the emergency notifications to the stakeholders.

For more information on emergency notifications, see [[integrating-crisis-mgmt-with-everbridge-uib|Emergency notifications in Everbridge]] and [[create-emergency-notification-uib-ws|Create emergency notifications]]. For information on the setup tasks for Everbridge, see [[setup-steps-for-emergency-notification-uib-ws|Setup for Everbridge notifications]].

-   **[[workflow-status-of-emergency-notification-uib-ws|Status of emergency notifications]]**  
When you send an emergency notification for a crisis event with Everbridge, it creates a corresponding incident along with a notification. You can track the notification delivery and monitor its status in the Business Continuity Workspace until it’s successfully delivered to the contacts from Everbridge.
-   **[Create emergency notifications](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/governance-risk-compliance/create-emergency-notification-uib-ws.md)**  
Create an emergency notification in BCM UIB Workspace. You can then use Everbridge to notify the stakeholders.
