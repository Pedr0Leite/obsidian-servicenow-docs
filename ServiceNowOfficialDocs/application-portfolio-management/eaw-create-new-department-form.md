# Create department form

Create a business unit to define your organizational functions.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Name|Name of the department.|
|ID|A unique identification number of the department|
|Department head|The person who heads the department.|
|Primary contact|The person who is the primary point of contact for anything associated with the department.|
|Description|A description of the department|
