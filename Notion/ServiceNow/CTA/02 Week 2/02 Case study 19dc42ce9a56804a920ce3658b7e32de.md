### **Recap**

Last week you learned about the role of the Certified Technical Architect. You familiarized yourself with Sovereign Bank and their desired outcomes. Additionally, you presented a summary of the challenges they are likely to face and how you plan to overcome them.

---

**Objective**

Applying what you learned in this week's material, articulate the value of implementing the CMDB, and using the CSDM framework, to resistant stakeholders to meet Sovereign Bank's transformation objectives and generate the expected business outcomes.

Given Sovereign Bank already has some information on their application/services portfolio, align the content to the Common Services Data Model (CSDM) so that the stakeholders and relationships can be identified in all ServiceNow processes: e.g., identification of service owners in Change Management to approve a change.

Your audience will be Li Na (Enterprise Architect) and the Configuration Manager.

### **Desired business outcomes:**

- A centralized best-in-class CMDB.
- Data certification, architectural standards, and a health dashboard.
- Simplification and automation of processes across the enterprise.
- Extendibility of the platform across the Sovereign Bank Group.

### **Key factors to consider:**

- Sovereign Bank has a single instance running HR. It is not being used to its full potential and is limited to “General HR Inquiry.”
- Sovereign Bank was using Remedy, which is highly customized, that holds the CIs, updated through an internally developed change management system, and discovered through a variety of third-party tools.
- Due to length of time between changes in technology, significant resistance to change is expected from some areas of the ITSM community within Sovereign Bank (specifically process owners and process managers, along with senior key helpdesk staff).
- Sovereign Bank leadership needs to know what input and guidance (and from whom) are needed to implement a CMDB with ITOM.

---

**Discuss with your small group:**

- What are the critical IT and business gaps, related to the CMDB, to resolve for Sovereign Bank?
- What are the biggest challenges you may encounter?
- What did you learn from Suzi about mapping concepts to real ServiceNow models to find the most correct approach?
- Where could you identify and capture quick win opportunities?
