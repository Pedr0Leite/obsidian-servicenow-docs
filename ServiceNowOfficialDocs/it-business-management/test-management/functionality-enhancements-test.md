# Functionality enhancements in Test Management 2.0

[[test-management2-landing-page|Test Management 2.0]] offers a few functionality enhancements over [[c_TestManagement|Test Management 1.0]].

## Integration with Agile Development 2.0

Integration of Test Management 2.0 with [[agile-landing-page|Agile Development 2.0]] provides embedded testing capabilities for scrum teams. Test Management 2.0 enables you to create [[c_Tests|tests]] for stories of the sprint, and also track the execution and completion of those tests. As a scrum lead or product owner, you gain an insight of the stories that are tested and are to be closed.
