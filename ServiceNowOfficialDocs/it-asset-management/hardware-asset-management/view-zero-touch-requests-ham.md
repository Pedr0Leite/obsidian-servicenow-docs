# View hardware asset requests submitted through the Zero Touch request flow

View the list of hardware asset requests of your employees submitted through the [[zero-touch-request|Zero Touch request flow]] in the [[asset-operations-view|Asset operations view]].

## Before you begin

Role required: admin or asset

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **[[using-ham-workspace|Hardware Asset Workspace]]** &gt; **Asset operations**.

2.  From the **Zero touch** list, select **Asset requests**.

    The list of asset requests is displayed. You can get the request details such as requested item, quantity, and stage.

3.  [[select-daas-asset|Select an asset]] request to view the details.
