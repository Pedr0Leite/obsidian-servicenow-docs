# Contract management view

Use the Contract management view in the Asset Workspace to view and manage details of [[c_Contracts|contracts]] such as Contract number, Contract start and end dates, [[c_TermsAndConditions|terms and conditions]] statements.

The Contract management view includes important actions for managing your contract, contract expenditure by type and vendor, and the list of expiring contracts.
