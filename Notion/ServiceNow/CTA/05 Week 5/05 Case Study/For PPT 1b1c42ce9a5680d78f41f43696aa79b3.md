# For PPT

## **1. Presentation Structure**

### **Title:**

**Sovereign Bank: Data Strategy, Governance, and Instance Replication**

### **Audience:**

- **Albert Wan (Platform Owner)** – Focus on governance policies.
- **Li Na (Enterprise Architect/Platform Architect)** – Focus on data replication strategy.

---

## **2. Key Topics & Talking Points**

### **1. Data Strategy for Sovereign Bank**

🔹 **Goal:** A simple, flexible, reliable, and secure data strategy to support business needs.

🔹 **Key Considerations:**

- **Centralization** vs. **Decentralization** for multi-region operations.
- **Data Integrity & Compliance:** GDPR, Privacy Act AU, PDPA APAC.
- **Historical Data Strategy:** Storage of 5 years of legacy data from Remedy.
- **Integration with HR Systems:** SAP SuccessFactors (EMEA/Asia) & Workday (North America).
- **Data Access Control:** Ensure only authorized access while maintaining compliance.

📌 **Recommendation:** Implement a **hybrid data model** with structured domain separation and controlled replication.

---

### **2. Architectural Model for Data Strategy**

🔹 **Three Approaches:**

- **Multi-instance architecture** (Each region has a separate instance).
- **Domain separation within a single instance** (Controlled data segregation).
- **Hybrid approach** (Combination of multi-instance + domain separation).

📌 **Recommendation:** **Hybrid Approach**

- **Single global instance with domain separation** for general operations.
- **Dedicated UK-based instance** for **UK customer data** to comply with data residency laws.
- **Federated Data Model:** Data is available based on roles and regional governance.

---

### **3. Data Migration & Integration Strategy**

🔹 **Scope of Migration:**

- **5 Years of historical data** (Incidents, Changes, Knowledge, Attachments).
- **Migration Sources:** Remedy (legacy ITSM), SAP SuccessFactors, Workday.
- **Migration Methods:** Delimited Flat Files, JSON, Web Service Push.
- **Data Normalization & Transformation:** Ensure consistency across different platforms.

📌 **Recommendation:**

- **Staging area in ServiceNow** for initial cleansing & validation.
- **Incremental migration strategy** to reduce downtime.
- **Data archiving strategy** for long-term compliance.

---

### **4. Instance Data Replication Strategy**

🔹 **Key Considerations:**

- **Synchronization of global data** across multiple instances.
- **Ensuring a single source of truth** while meeting regional compliance.
- **Defining what needs replication:** HR, CMDB, ITSM records.
- **Handling high-volume data replication:** Incremental vs. full replication.

📌 **Recommendation:**

- **Use ServiceNow MID Servers & Data Replication Application** for controlled sync.
- **Leverage API-based integration** for real-time data sharing where necessary.
- **Prioritize replication for:**1️⃣ HR records2️⃣ CMDB & Asset data3️⃣ Active tickets & cases4️⃣ Historical case & product data (batch processing).

---

### **5. Data Governance & Compliance Strategy**

🔹 **Challenges:**

- **Different privacy laws in EMEA, APAC, and North America.**
- **Storage & retention policies** (Personal data in EU vs. AU vs. APAC).
- **Managing cross-instance access without violating regulations.**

📌 **Recommendation:**

- **Enforce strong access controls & encryption** (Regional-based access policies).
- **Data Retention Policy:**
    - **EU:** Personal data retained per GDPR (Right to be forgotten).
    - **AU & APAC:** Align with local privacy acts.
    - **UK:** Store UK customer data within a UK data center.
- **Auditing & Logging:** Automate compliance reporting.

---

### **6. Priority of Instance Data Replication & Roadmap**

🔹 **Phased Approach for Replication:**

- **Phase 1:** HR & CMDB Data
- **Phase 2:** Live Incident, Change, and Case Data
- **Phase 3:** Historical Data Migration
- **Phase 4:** Full ITSM Service Replication

📌 **Outcome:** Minimize impact, ensure compliance, and create a scalable solution for Sovereign Bank’s data needs.

---

## **3. Presentation Notes (What to Say for Each Slide)**

### **Slide 1: Introduction**

"Good morning, Albert and Li Na. Today, we’ll outline Sovereign Bank’s data strategy, governance, and replication approach. Our goal is to ensure a scalable, compliant, and efficient data architecture as the bank evolves."

### **Slide 2: Data Strategy**

"Sovereign Bank needs a structured but flexible data approach to ensure compliance, security, and operational efficiency. The focus is on **consolidating historical data, integrating HR systems, and meeting compliance requirements** without disrupting operations."

### **Slide 3: Architectural Model**

"We considered **three architectural options**—multi-instance, domain separation, and a hybrid approach. Given your needs, we recommend a **hybrid model** with domain separation for controlled access and a dedicated UK instance for UK data residency compliance."

### **Slide 4: Data Migration Strategy**

"With 5 years of historical data, a **structured migration plan** is crucial. We propose an **incremental migration strategy** using staging areas for cleansing, and phased data transfers via **flat files, JSON, and web services** to reduce risk."

### **Slide 5: Instance Data Replication**

"Synchronization of key data elements is necessary for a **single source of truth**. We propose an **API-based integration & MID Server approach** to manage real-time and batch replication of **HR, CMDB, ITSM, and historical data** in priority phases."

### **Slide 6: Data Governance & Compliance**

"Different privacy regulations across EMEA, APAC, and North America require **strict access control and data residency enforcement**. We recommend an **audited, encrypted, and region-based access policy** with automated compliance monitoring."

### **Slide 7: Roadmap & Priorities**

"Our roadmap prioritizes **HR and CMDB first**, followed by **live case data, historical migration, and full ITSM service replication**. This ensures minimal disruption while progressively improving Sovereign Bank’s data ecosystem."

### **Slide 8: Conclusion & Next Steps**

"By adopting this approach, Sovereign Bank can **securely consolidate its data, maintain compliance, and ensure smooth operations**. Next steps include **finalizing data governance policies, implementing integration priorities, and preparing for phased execution**."
