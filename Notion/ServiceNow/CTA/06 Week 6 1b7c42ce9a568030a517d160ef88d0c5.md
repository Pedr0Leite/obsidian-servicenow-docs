Now that you are in week 6, take a moment to reflect on your progress so far. Watch your personal objective video from the Welcome week. Then, consider the following questions:

1. How’s it going with the personal objectives you set at the beginning of the program? A: I’ve been learning so much with all the other people, it’s been such an amazing ride!
I’ve also been able to use the content of the course to adapt to my own reality and start to see ways of improving and training my brain to ask before acting! That was always my major goal for the CTA!
2. What would you like to change or add now that you are in the middle of your Certified Technical Architect program journey? A: Sometimes I would love to have more time on friday calls so we can all discuss the topics a bit deeper with personal experiences!
3. What are some activities you could start or stop doing to ensure you meet your goals? A: I need to stop feeling like I’m not up to the task of becoming a CTA and let my self dive deep in this amazing experience. Is by far my favorite ServiceNow course so far!
