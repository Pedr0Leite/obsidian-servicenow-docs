# Modify BPMN diagram details

Business Process Model and Notation \(BPMN\) diagram element directly from the diagram page. You can make quick updates without leaving the modeling interface.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_apm.apm\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **[[ea-workspace|Enterprise Architecture Workspace]]**.

2.  Open the Modeling page by selecting the Modeling icon \(\[Omitted image "icon-modeling-logo.png"\] Alt text:\).

3.  Open an existing BPMN diagram.

4.  Select the Modify business process details icon on the diagram page.

    \[Omitted image "modeling-modify-bpmn.png"\] Alt text: Modify business process details icon on the BPMN diagram page

5.  Modify the details such as name, description, and other details.

    \[Omitted image "modeling-modify-bpmn-details.png"\] Alt text: Modifying the details of a business process

6.  Select **Update**.
