# Touchpoints

Plan, log, and track customer interactions throughout the engagement lifecycle using touchpoint records.

[[account-lifecycle-touchpoints|Touchpoints]] capture interactions between your team and customers throughout the engagement lifecycle. Use touchpoint records to plan meetings, log conversations, manage related tasks, and maintain a complete history of customer engagement.

-   **[[account-lifecycle-touchpoint-home|Touchpoint home page]]**  
During the engagement lifecycle, [[account-lifecycle-events-customer-success-about|customer success]] agents schedule regular touchpoints with customers to evaluate progress, provide feedback, and offer guidance.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-touchpoint-planner|Touchpoint planner]]**  
The touchpoint planner shows a calendar view of different touchpoints across all accounts associated with the customer success manager.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-zoom-meeting-summary|Generate summary for Zoom meetings]]**  
Generate the meeting summary for virtual meetings conducted using Zoom.
