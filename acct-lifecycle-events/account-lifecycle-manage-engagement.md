# Engagements

Configure engagement health scores, risk definitions, and the color banding table to monitor and visualize the health and risk of [[account-lifecycle-engagement|engagements]].

-   **[[account-lifecycle-setup-health-defn|Calculate the engagement health score]]**  
Use the **Calculated** metric data source to determine the health score of an engagement.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-setup-risk-defn|Define risk criteria]]**  
Use the engagement risk definition to configure the metrics required to define the risk signals for an engagement.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-setup-color-banding|Set up the color banding table]]**  
Use the color banding table to set up how the engagement health score is visually represented in the Success Landing and Engagement pages.
