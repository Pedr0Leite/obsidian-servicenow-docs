# Configure customer success

[[account-lifecycle-events-customer-success-about|Customer success]] capabilities help you monitor and manage the health of customer accounts throughout their lifecycle. Configure these capabilities to track [[account-lifecycle-engagement|engagements]], identify risks, and support retention goals.

-   **[[account-lifecycle-basic-config|Getting started with Customer Success]]**  
Define the basic [[account-lifecycle-use-cust-success|customer success]] components
-   **[[account-lifecycle-manage-engagement|Engagements]]**  
Configure engagement health scores, risk definitions, and the color banding table to monitor and visualize the health and risk of engagements.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-setup-success-blueprints|Configure success blueprints]]**  
Configure [[account-lifecycle-success-blueprint|success blueprint]] templates and the supporting elements that customer success managers use to define how success is measured for an engagement.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-setup-prod-cap|Configure product capabilities]]**  
Configure product capabilities, adoption roadmap templates, and capability usage tracking to measure how customers use your products.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-setup-zoom-int|Configure Zoom and touchpoint integration]]**  
Integrate Zoom with touchpoint records to schedule virtual meetings and generate AI-powered meeting summaries.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-crm-integration|Enable integration with Microsoft Dynamics CRM]]**  
Integrating your ServiceNow instance with the Microsoft Dynamics CRM platform enables seamless data exchange and process synchronization between the two platforms. This integration is done through the Microsoft Dynamics CRM spoke.
