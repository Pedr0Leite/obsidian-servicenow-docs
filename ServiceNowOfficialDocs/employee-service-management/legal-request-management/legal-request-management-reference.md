# Legal Request Management reference

[[reference-hr-service-delivery-advanced-integration-with-workday|Reference]] topics provide additional information about the lists and forms that you use in [[legal-request-management-landing-page|Legal Request Management]].

-   **[Legal Service Delivery properties](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/legal-properties.md)**  
There are several properties that you can use to configure integrations for [[legal-management-overview|Legal Service Delivery]] apps.
-   **[Components installed with Legal Request Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/installed-with-legal-request-management.md)**  
Several types of components are installed with activation of the Legal Request Management application, including tables and user roles.
-   **[Practice area tables for legal requests](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/legal-request-pa-tables.md)**  
Tables specific to practice areas are installed with the Legal Request Management application.
-   **[Legal request record - Details tab](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/legal-request-fields.md)**  
List of fields in the **Details** tab of a legal request in the [[legal-counsel-center-landing|Legal Counsel Center]].
-   **[Response Template form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/response-template-fields.md)**  
Legal configurator can use the [[response-template-fields|Response Template form]] to create or modify a response template for reusable messages.
