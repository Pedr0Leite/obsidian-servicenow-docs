# Manage Enterprise Modeling and Visualization

Enterprise Modeling and Visualization refers to the modeling and diagramming tools within the EA Workspace to represent business capabilities, business hierarchies, and their interrelationships.

The Enterprise Modeling and Visualization diagrams aren’t illustrative but interactive artifacts that support planning and governance with regard to your [[application-portfolio-management-landing-page|enterprise architecture]] landscape. You can create, edit, and manage diagrams using ArchiMate, general, or [[eaw-modeling-ea-shapes|Enterprise Architecture shapes]].
