# Employee experience using Employee Center

Simplify meeting employee needs by leveraging the automated self-service and browsing experience in the Employee Center portal.

## Overview and key features of the Employee Center portal

From the Employee Center portal, an employee can perform the following actions:

-   Search for relevant information. In the base system, the following external content sources are supported:
    -   Microsoft Support/Microsoft Windows
    -   Zoom
    -   Webex
    -   Okta
    -   Apple Support
    -   Zscaler
-   Open IT ticket
-   Check the status of employee's requests and incidents in a simplified view
-   Browse commonly requested catalog items
-   Access open and completed tasks
-   Access the requests assigned to or submitted by the employee
-   Access the favorites
-   Review notifications
-   Get support

**Note:** The Employee Center portal experience is integrated and available in Microsoft Teams and Slack chat.
