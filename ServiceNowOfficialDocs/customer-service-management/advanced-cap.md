# Extend capabilities

To extend your [[c_CustomerServiceManagement|Customer Service Management]] environment, integrate additional applications and features. This enhances functionalities and improves support for more efficient interactions.

\[Omitted image "workflow-extend-cap.png"\] Alt text: overview of topics within the extend capabilities category

|Application or feature|Description|
|----------------------|-----------|
|[[outsourced-service-provider|Outsourced Customer Service]]|Configure Outsourced Customer Service to enable the outsourced company or individuals to perform tasks, handle operations, or provide services.|
|[[configure-pcso|Proactive Customer Service Operations]]|Configure [[proactive-service-operations|Proactive Customer Service Operations]] to trigger case workflows and notify customers of any issues to resolve issues faster and lower inbound call volume.|
|[[configure-pa-r|Configure Customer Service Platform Analytics Solutions]]|Configure [[customer-service-content-pack|Customer Service Platform Analytics Solutions]] to view preconfigured dashboards with actionable data visualizations to improve your business processes.|
|[[ssa-solution-config|Configure the Platform Analytics Solution for Self-Service Analytics for Customer Service]]|Configure Platform Analytics Solution for Self-Service Analytics to run diagnostics, review and customize components, and start collecting data.|
|[Continual Improvement Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/it-service-management/cim-landing-page.md)|Request improvement opportunities, and implement phases and tasks to meet performance goals, track progress, and measure success.|
|[Process Mining](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/now-intelligence/process-mining.md)|Create automated business process flows from your data to help analysts and process owners to analyze and optimize their business processes.|
|[[configurable-wfo-cs|Workforce Optimization for Customer Service]]|Manage and maintain the productivity of your workforce and efficiently route work to your team, manage your team's skills and schedules, and monitor their performance.|
