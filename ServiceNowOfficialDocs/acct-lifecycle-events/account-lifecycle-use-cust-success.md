# Customer success

Learn how your organization can use the [[account-lifecycle-events-customer-success-about|customer success]] solution to engage with your customers, mitigate risks by running success plays, and identify opportunities for expansion and renewals.

**Important:** Account Lifecycle Events has been renamed to [[account-lifecycle-events-landing|Customer Success Management]] in Zurich.

-   **[[account-lifecycle-dashboards|Dashboards]]**  
Monitor engagement health, portfolio performance, and risk trends using the customer success dashboards.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-manage-engage|Manage engagements]]**  
Create and manage customer [[account-lifecycle-engagement|engagements]], success blueprints, [[account-lifecycle-touchpoints|touchpoints]], risk signals, and success plays throughout the customer success journey.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-manage-cust-succ-playbooks|Manage customer success playbooks]]**  
Use the [[account-lifecycle-cust-succ-playbook|customer success playbooks]] to manage planned and unplanned activities during an engagement. Configure the structure of these playbooks, including their stages and activities, use the Playbook builder.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-use-product-adopt|Product adoption]]**  
Track how customers use your products and plan their adoption journey using product capability records and adoption roadmaps.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-use-touchpoints|Touchpoints]]**  
Plan, log, and track customer interactions throughout the engagement lifecycle using touchpoint records.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-view-customer-success-portal|View customer success cases in the CSM portal]]**  
View the customer success case records or case task records on the Customer Service Management \(CSM\) portal.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-cust-success-related-items|View related items]]**  
The Related Items component provides access to related lists associated with [[account-lifecycle-manage-engagement|engagements]], customer plays, and internal plays
-   **[[account-lifecycle-ai-search|AI search with recommended actions]]**  
Use AI search with recommended actions to create contexts and search for relevant records.
