# Export a survey scorecard as an image

You can export a scorecard as an image to use in presentations or other documents.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin or survey\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Click the menu icon \(\[Omitted image "Menu.png"\] Alt text: Menu icon\) and select **Save as PNG** or **Save as JPEG** and wait for the export to complete.

    \[Omitted image "SurveyScorecardExportImage.png"\] Alt text: Scorecard Export Image

2.  Click **Download** and save the scorecard image to a storage location.
