# Account onboarding

With the [[account-lifecycle-playbook-overview|account onboarding]] solution, service providers can repeatedly define, measure, and collaborate with customers on the activities needed to onboard rapidly.

**Important:** Account Lifecycle Events has been renamed to [[account-lifecycle-events-landing|Customer Success Management]] in Zurich.

-   **[[account-lifecycle-events-onboarding-landing|Account onboarding landing page]]**  
Get real-time visibility into your onboarding cases and track your onboarding tasks.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-manage-acct-onboard|Manage account onboarding cases]]**  
Work through the activities in the [[account-lifecycle-account-onboard-playbook|account onboarding playbook]] to manage an account onboarding case from creation through go-live and post-support.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-view-csm-portal|View account onboarding cases in the CSM portal]]**  
View the account onboarding case records or case task records on the Customer Service Management \(CSM\) portal.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-oob-rec|Recommended actions available with account onboarding]]**  
Use the predefined recommended actions that are available with the base system during the account onboarding process.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-create-onboard-success-play|Create a success play for account onboarding]]**  
Use this option to [[account-lifecycle-create-success-play|create a success play]] for successfully completing an account onboarding journey.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-related-items|View related items]]**  
The Related Items component provides access to related list items associated with your account onboarding case.
-   **[[account-lifecycle-knowledge-base|Account onboarding knowledge base]]**  
Several knowledge base articles are provided for account onboarding.
