# Regulatory assessment for a regulatory alert

Utilize the [[smart-asmnt-engine-landing-page|Smart Assessment Engine]] to perform smart assessments on [[list-view-of-reg-alerts|regulatory alerts]]. This ability enhances regulatory decision-making by enabling impact assessments directly at the regulatory alert level, streamlining processes through a unified core assessment framework, and assigning analysis to multiple stakeholders for improved collaboration and efficiency.

When you initiate an assessment to [[impact-assessment-tasks|assess the impact of a regulatory alert]], you get an option select either to start a regulatory assessment or the classic risk assessment. Regulatory assessment utilizes the Smart Assessment Engine application and when it is initiated, a new related list called **Regulatory assessments** is available on a regulatory alert that displays the list of uniquely generated regulatory assessments. The assessor can access the assessment from the unified **Tasks** page on the [[employee-center|Employee Center]]. The unified **Tasks** page provides you a consolidated view of all your tasks, including all assessments, enabling you to access and complete them efficiently. For more information on how to perform a regulatory assessment, refer to [Respond to a regulatory assessment](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/governance-risk-compliance/regulatory-change-management-service-portal/respond-to-a-regulatory-assessment.md).

To understand the benefits of using the Smart Assessment Engine, refer to [Exploring Smart Assessment Engine](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/governance-risk-compliance/smart-assessment-engine/smart-assessment-engine-cf-explore.md).

To understand the process of creating [[airc-assessment-templates|assessment templates]] and questionnaires, refer to [Configuring Smart Assessment Engine](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/governance-risk-compliance/smart-assessment-engine/smart-assessment-engine-cf-config.md).

To understand how to initiate assessments, see [Assess the impact of a regulatory alert](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/governance-risk-compliance/regulatory-change-management-service-portal/impact-assessment-tasks.md).
