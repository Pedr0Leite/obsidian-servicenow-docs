# For PPT

[week 1 presentation.pptx](For%20PPT/week_1_presentation.pptx)

**Regulatory Compliance**

- Implementing fine-grained access controls to restrict data visibility by region.
- Mapping regulatory requirements to ServiceNow features (e.g., which fields must be encrypted per law).
- Ensuring continuous monitoring & governance to stay compliant with evolving regulations.

**Organizational & Cultural Challenges**

- Resistance to Change
- Business Unit Alignment & Process Standardization
- Training & Adoption

**Data Security and Privacy Challenges**

- Enforcing the right balance between security & usability (e.g., encrypted fields may limit reporting capabilities).
- Ensuring that all integrations respect security requirements (e.g., legacy HR and Finance systems need encryption).
- Managing access control for multi-regional teams while following data segregation policies.

**Legacy Systems Integration Challenges**

- **Managing complex authentication needs** across multiple systems (SSO, ADFS, MFA).
- **Navigating firewall and security restrictions** for cloud-to-on-premise integrations.
- **Replacing or modernizing legacy applications** that **lack modern integration capabilities**.

**Operational Disruption Challenges**

- **Managing parallel system operations** during transition (both Remedy & ServiceNow running).
- **Ensuring minimal downtime for critical banking services** during migration.
- **Handling resistance from employees** who prefer the **old system and manual processes**

**Change Management Challenges**

- **Aligning multiple business units** under a **single standardized Change Management process**.
- **Convincing risk-averse teams** to embrace automation.
- **Preventing unauthorized workarounds** (e.g., manually bypassing CAB approvals).

**Complexity and Scale Challenges**

- **Coordinating governance across multiple regions** with different IT policies.
- **Ensuring performance scalability** as new teams and applications onboard.
- **Handling high user adoption rates without overwhelming support teams**.
