# Unassign a business application from a capability

You can unassign a business application from a capability.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_apm.apm\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **[[ea-workspace|Enterprise Architecture Workspace]]**.

2.  Open the Business Capability Hierarchy page by selecting the Business Portfolio icon \(\[Omitted image "business-portfolio-icon.png"\] Alt text: Business Portfolio icon\).

3.  Select the expand row icon \(\[Omitted image "ExpandIcon.png"\] Alt text: Expand Row icon.\) next to the relevant business capability.

4.  Select the row context menu icon \(\[Omitted image "icon-menu.png"\] Alt text: Row context menu icon.\) next to the relevant business application and select **Unassign business application**.
