# Invite a new member to a group feed

The feed administrator can invite another user or a group to join a feed.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## About this task

When a team is added to a feed, all members of the team also indirectly become members of the feed. Only users, and not teams, can be the feed administrators. A team member who is following a feed can become administrator by directly inviting another user to the feed.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  Click either **Group Feeds** or **[[c_RecordFeeds|Record Feeds]]**, and then select the feed name.

3.  Click the **Pending members** tab.

4.  In the **Add user or team** field, begin entering a user or team name and select a user or team from the suggestion list.

    The invited user or team members receive an [[email|email]] notification.
