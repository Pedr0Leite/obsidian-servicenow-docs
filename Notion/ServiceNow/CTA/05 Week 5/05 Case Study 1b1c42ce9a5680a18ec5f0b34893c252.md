# 05. Case Study

### **Recap**

Last week you created a technical governance policy for Sovereign Bank to drive effective decision making including their release and instance strategy. You also identified the appropriate agile framework to use for their go-forward operating model.

---

### **Objective:**

Create a data strategy for Sovereign Bank to minimize impact to their services as they grow in their data usage and utilization across their enterprise.

Review the case study and prepare a data governance policy for the platform owner and data replication strategy for the platform architect.

Your audience will be Albert Wan (Platform Owner) and  Li Na (Enterprise Architect).

(**Note**: The enterprise architect is taking on the responsibilities of the platform architect in this scenario.)

### **Desired business outcomes:**

- All historical customer case and product data in one platform.
- Integration with both HR systems of record.
- 5 years of historical data available from legacy platform, including all incidents, changes, knowledge articles, and other attachments.
- Understanding of a migration strategy for data such as tickets, tasks, knowledge articles, CMDB etc. via delimited flat files, JSON, web service push.
- Any data normalization/transformation included as part of the project.

### **Key factors to consider:**

- Multiple HR systems of record: SAP SuccessFactors HR (EMEA and Asia), and Workday (North America).
- How, where and for how long can they store data labelled as personal in order to comply with the European Commission General Data Protection Regulation in the EU, Privacy Act in Australia, and Personal Data Protection Act 2012 in Asia Pacific?
- The customer data of UK residents must be stored in a data center that is UK-based.
- Remedy is used as the bank’s ITSM solution.

---

**Discuss:**

- What architectural model is best for Sovereign Bank’s requirement of having multiple systems spread across different regions?
- What are the data structures and volumes that need to be migrated?
- How do you address multiple systems of record?
- How do you solve data storage to comply with different regional regulations?
- How do you migrate and where to store 5 years’ worth of historical case and product data?
- How will Sovereign Bank share data between ServiceNow instances (if/where needed) and automatically keep that data in sync?
- How will Sovereign Bank propagate data across multiple instances so that they maintain a single source of truth?
- What are the data sources needed to be replicated?
- What are the source and target instances?
- What needs normalization/transformation?
- How much data is there to replicate?
- What level of complexity is the data that needs to be integrated?
