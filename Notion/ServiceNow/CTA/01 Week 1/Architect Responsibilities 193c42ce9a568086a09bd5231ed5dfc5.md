# Architect Responsibilities

![image.png](Architect%20Responsibilities/image.png)

The Certified Technical Architect (CTA) is a valued partner and trusted technical advisor on an organization's digital transformation journey.

The CTA uses technical leading practices and optimizes the technical health of an organization's ServiceNow solution.

## **Experience**

Certified Technical Architects have a successful track record in management consulting or professional services for cloud software companies. They also have experience with technology strategy, governance, data and solution design, and architecture. They’re trusted advisors that facilitate customer success across strategic planning functions from business value identification and road mapping to advising and guiding successful execution plans.

## **Essential functions**

- Understands customer’s vision and how ServiceNow supports customer’s digital transformation journey. Guides how business objectives/outcomes can be achieved through technical solutions.
- Analyzes and translates business and technical requirements into an architectural blueprint.
- Creates and manages technical governance processes.
- Drives solid instance health by reviewing and guiding the remediation of configurations that do not align to ServiceNow leading practices.
- Establishes delivery operating model governance to ensure successful go-lives, end-user adoption, and on-going support.
- Guides customer down a prescriptive solution design and manages through the technical implementation decision points.
- Defines solutions across the platform that align to out of the box (OOB) capabilities.
- Understands the importance of organizational change management (OCM) for customer success. To learn more, review [How do I start organizational change management?](https://www.servicenow.com/content/dam/servicenow-assets/public/en-us/doc-type/success/quick-answer/organizational-change-management-begin.pdf)

## **Core competencies**

- Customer focus
- Collaborative
- Drives results
- Cultivates innovation
- Consultative perspective
- Manages complexity
- Strategic mindset
- Technical development
