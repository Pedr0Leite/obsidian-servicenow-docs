# Introducing AI Agents and Quick Start Guide

### **AI Agents: Unlock autonomous productivity at scale with Now Assist and the Now Platform**

By now you've undoubtedly heard of Agentic AI powered by large language models that autonomously plans, reasons, and takes proactive actions. It has the ability to transform automation from pre-scripted workflows to dynamic problem-solving. This exciting innovation now comes natively to the ServiceNow Platform in our latest release. In the Yokohama release, AI Agents is available to all customers who have Now Assist!

**How AI Agents work on the Now Platform**

**Prerequisites and Getting Started**

- Now Assist license
- Yokohama Patch 1+ or Xanadu Patch 7+ (Latest patch recommended.)
- Now Assist for IT, HRSD..., and AI Agents store apps installed. Make sure dependency apps are also installed and updated.
- AI Search enabled
- Now Assist Panel turned on

Once the prerequisites are fulfilled, in your instance, add role: **sn_aia.admin**, then navigate to **All > AI Agent Studio > Overview,** to access the AI Agents Studio. In the Studio, you can create Use Cases, AI Agents, and tools using natural language prompts. AI Agents can contain multiple tools, and Use Cases can contain multiple AI Agents. Tools include:

- Subflows/Actions
- Script
- Skills (Out-of-box or via Skill Kit)
- RAG (AI Search)
- ...and more!

You can then test your Agentic Workflows and see the complete chain of thought before deploying it.
