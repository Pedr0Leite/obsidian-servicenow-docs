# Add Covered Products using Contracts and Entitlement Workflow

Covered products refers to specific products or order line items that are included in a customer contract. These items are linked to the contract to define what is covered for service or maintenance. Covered products can be directly tracked through the contract records, facilitating changes and visibility of what is covered.

Agents in [[explore-order-management|Order management]] can select the products to be covered by a contract when the order is captured for the contract offering. To manually associate a covered product with a contract line, see the [[som-update-covered-products|Add covered products to order line items]]

You can also [[Modify|modify]] the covered products added to a contract line. You can add new covered products to that contract line or entitlement and remove the existing covered products.
