# What are your personal objectives?

# 

Hello, my name is Pedro Leite and my main personal objectives for the CTA program is to become a better at translating customer needs into ServiceNow products and solutions.

Currently, I'm a strong technically but I sometimes lack the training and knowledge on how to better transform and access client requirements into valid solutions.

Sometimes, I don’t know which product might be better for a certain problem that a client has and I lack the experience in designing a whole solution to present via non-technical presentation.

I tend to always do prof of concepts while I want to also know how to better advise the client in a “Bigger picture” kind of way.

I also want to be able to structure all the phases correctly in order to know which products might come first depending on the client requirements
