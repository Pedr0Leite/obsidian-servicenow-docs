# Exploring ITOM/OT SU Licensing

Learn more about ServiceNow® ITOM/OT SU Licensing, the different ITOM subscriptions it includes, and its benefit.

## ITOM/OT SU Licensing overview

The ITOM/OT SU Licensing application calculates and displays how many subscriptions for [[r_ITOMApplications|IT Operations Management]] \(ITOM\) applications your organization purchases and consumes. Subscriptions for the following ITOM applications are available: ServiceNow® [[itom-visibility-landing-page|ITOM Visibility]], ServiceNow® ITOM [[r-discovery|Discovery]], ServiceNow® [[itom-health-landing-page|ITOM AIOps]], ServiceNow® [[hla-landing-page|Health Log Analytics]], ServiceNow® [[itom-optimization-landing-page|ITOM Optimization]], ServiceNow® [[itom-cloud-accelerate-landing-page|ITOM Cloud Accelerate]], ServiceNow® Digital End-User Experience \(DEX\), and ServiceNow® [[service-observability|Service Observability]]. You can purchase subscriptions for each ITOM application separately \(a la carte\) or together \(in a bundle\).

|Benefit|Feature|Users|
|-------|-------|-----|
|Viewing the usage of ITOM applications to make informed decisions about your ITOM subscriptions.
