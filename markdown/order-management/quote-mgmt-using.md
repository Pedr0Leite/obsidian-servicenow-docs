# Using Quote Management

Use the [[quote-management|Quote Management]] application to build your quote. The interface includes the following tabs to help you create and manage product quotes.

|Quote Management tab|Description|
|--------------------|-----------|
|Details tab|Add details of the quote, such as address and contact information.|
|Line Items tab|Add quote line items to a quote.|
|Catalog tab|Use product catalogs to add products to your quote.|
|Revisions tab|Create multiple versions of quotes.|
|Customer Orders tab|Convert quotes to product order sales.|
|Pricing adjustment tab|Add manual pricing adjustments to products in a quote.|

## Building quotes

Use these basic steps to create, build, and manage your quotes.

|Step|Description|
|----|-----------|
|[[create-new-quote|Create a quote]]|Start a quote, then [[quote-management-details-tab|add and view quote details.]]|
|[[quote-management-catalog-tab|Add products to a quote]]|Build your quote by adding products from the product catalogs.|
|[[quote-create-pdf-document|Create a quote PDF document]]|Sales agents can create a PDF document from a quote to send to customers.|
|[[quote-management-line-items-tab|View and update products in your quote]]-|Use the Line items tab to see and update products in your quote.|
|[[quote-management-pricing-adjustments-tab|View and update manual pricing adjustments in quotes]]|Add pricing adjustments to quotes.|
|[[quote-get-product-offer|Get additional product offering recommendations for quotes]]|Use product offering recommendations to find other products that you can upsell or cross-sell to your customers based on the line items in your quote.|
|[[quote-management-revisions-tab|Create quote versions]]|Use the Revisions tab to create and view versions of a quote.|
|[[quote-management-customer-orders-tab|Convert quotes into orders]]|Use the Customer Orders tab to convert quotes to orders.|

-   **[Create a quote](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/order-management/create-new-quote.md)**  
Create a quote in Quote Management to start the quote building process.
-   **[Add and view details of a quote](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/order-management/quote-management-details-tab.md)**  
Use the Details tab to add and view basic information for a quote.
-   **[Add products to a quote](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/order-management/quote-management-catalog-tab.md)**  
Use the Catalog tab in Quote Management to add products to your quote.
-   **[[using-prod-recommendations-quote|Using product offering recommendations in quotes]]**  
As a sales agent, you can use recommended product offerings to upsell or cross-sell additional products in your quotes.
-   **[[quote-management-tasks|Add tasks to a quote]]**  
Create a single or multiple tasks for a quote and assign those to different agents. Tasks are the various actions that agents perform to create a quote.
-   **[Create quote versions](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/order-management/quote-management-revisions-tab.md)**  
Use the Revisions tab to create and view the versions of your quote.
-   **[Convert quotes into orders](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/order-management/quote-management-customer-orders-tab.md)**  
Use the Customer Order tab to convert a quote into a customer order in Quote Management.
-   **[[quote-management-add-pricing-adjustment|Add pricing adjustment to a quote line item]]**  
Add pricing adjustments to quote line items after the line item is created.
-   **[[add-price-ramps-on-a-quote-line-item|Add price ramps on a quote line item]]**  
Add price ramps to quote line items to define incremental price changes for recurring product offerings.
-   **[[add-header-discount-to-a-quote|Add a header discount to a quote]]**  
Apply a percentage-based discount to the entire quote, or to a selected type of line items within the quote. The quote line items automatically inherit the designated discount percentage as a manual adjustment on the quote line level.
-   **[[approving-or-rejecting-quotes|Approving or rejecting quotes]]**  
Sales agents request approval for quotes, and approvers decide whether quotes can proceed under an advanced approval workflow.
-   **[[quote-management-create-order-quote-line|Create an order from a quote line item]]**  
Create orders from quote line items by using the **Create new order** UI action.
-   **[[quote-management-add-subscription-pricing|Add subscription pricing to a quote]]**  
Add subscription pricing to a quote or quote line items including start and end dates in Quote Management.
-   **[[submit-a-quote-for-approval|Submit a quote for advanced approval]]**  
Submit a quote for advanced approval to route it to required approvers before sharing it with a customer.
-   **[[recall-a-quote-approval-request|Recall a quote approval request]]**  
[[cancel|Cancel]] a pending quote approval request to change a quote before resubmitting it for advanced approval.
-   **[[approve-a-quote-approval-request|Approve an approval request for a quote]]**  
Review and approve or reject a quote advanced approval request that is routed to you as part of an advanced approval workflow.
-   **[View and update products using the line items tab](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/order-management/quote-management-line-items-tab.md)**  
Use the line items tab to review and update products in your quote and to add additional line items.
-   **[View and update manual pricing adjustments in quotes](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/order-management/quote-management-pricing-adjustments-tab.md)**  
Use the Pricing Adjustments tab in Quote Management to view and update manual pricing adjustments made to order line items in your quote.
-   **[[quote-management-create-contract|Initiate a contract request]]**  
Initiate a contract request from the Quote Management application.
-   **[[quote-management-viewing-a-quote|View an existing quote]]**  
View existing quotes to update the quote status or make other changes.
-   **[[quote-management-export-quotes|Export quotes]]**  
Export a list of all quotes in Quote Management and provide it to a customer.
-   **[[quote-create-sales-agreement|Enable a sales agreement in a quote]]**  
Agents can enable a sales agreement for a quote. Once enabled in Quote Management, sales agreements are generated that show the product and service lines and other relevant details for the quote.
-   **[[som-sync-quote-with-opportunity|Sync a quote with an opportunity]]**  
Agents can sync quote information with an opportunity to ensure that information is consistent between both applications in Quote Management.
-   **[[som-quote-add-covered-products|Add covered products to a quote]]**  
Add a covered product to line items in a quote in Quote Management.
-   **[[quote-using-product-location|Filter catalogs by location in a quote]]**  
Use the location filter on the Catalog tab to filter products by location in Quote Management.
-   **[[quote-mgt-copy-quote-item-location|Copy locations for quote line items]]**  
Use the copy location function to change the location of multiple quote line items in Quote Management.
-   **[Create a quote PDF document](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/order-management/quote-create-pdf-document.md)**  
In Quote Management, sales agents can create a PDF of a quote that can be sent to customers for review and signatures.
-   **[[quote-view-a-quote-document|View a quote PDF document]]**  
Agents can view quote documents in the PDF viewer window. The PDF viewer window has controls that enable agents to manage the PDF.
-   **[[quote-management-view-hierarchical-line-items|Use hierarchical list view for quote line items]]**  
Sales agents can view quote line items as a hierarchical list, which helps to view the parent and child relationship between line items.
-   **[[consolidate-quotes|Consolidate quotes]]**  
Consolidate renewal quotes to merge multiple quotes for the same account into a single unified quote.
