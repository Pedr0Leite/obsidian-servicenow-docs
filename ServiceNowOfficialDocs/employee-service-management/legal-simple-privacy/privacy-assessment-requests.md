# Privacy assessment requests

Privacy assessment requests enables employees to seek services from the Legal Privacy department to resolve privacy-related concerns.

-   **[Submit a privacy assessment request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-simple-privacy/submit-legal-privacy-assessment-request.md)**  
[[submit-legal-privacy-assessment-request|Submit a privacy assessment request]] for yourself or on behalf of another user to seek services from the legal department.
-   **[Update a privacy assessment request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-simple-privacy/update-existing-legal-privacy-assessment-request.md)**  
Update request details, add comments, or upload documents in a privacy assessment request you submitted while it is still in the New or Assigned state.
-   **[Assign a privacy assessment request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-simple-privacy/assign-legal-privacy-assessment-request.md)**  
Assign a legal privacy assessment request to yourself or to someone in your assignment group.
-   **[Cancel a privacy assessment request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-simple-privacy/cancel-privacy-assessment-request.md)**  
[[cancel-privacy-assessment-request|Cancel a privacy assessment request]] if it is no longer necessary.
