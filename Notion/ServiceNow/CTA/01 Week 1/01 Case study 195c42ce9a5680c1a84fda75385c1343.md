# 01. Case study

### **Objective:**

Review the main case study and become familiar with Sovereign Bank, the program team, and Suzi Matsui, the lead Certified Technical Architect at ServiceWow Consulting Services (SCS).

Present a compelling argument for how your knowledge and skills as a technical architect will allow you and your team from SCS to overcome some of the challenges on the Sovereign Bank implementation. Your group can be a team at SCS or you can simply be part of SCS, without reference to your group.

Your audience will be the key players at Sovereign Bank:   Zhang Wie (Project Sponsor), Li Na (Enterprise Architect), and Albert Wan (Program Director) who will each be concerned with distinct aspects of the transformation.

---

**Discuss:**

- In your own role, what are your current architect responsibilities? How do they differ from the ones listed in the training materials?
- What knowledge and skills will be most useful in the Sovereign Bank implementation?
- What key challenges will test the team and require you to develop new skills?
- What key points should be included in your presentation?
- How can you structure your presentation to be most effective?
- What will your audience be looking to get out of the presentation?
