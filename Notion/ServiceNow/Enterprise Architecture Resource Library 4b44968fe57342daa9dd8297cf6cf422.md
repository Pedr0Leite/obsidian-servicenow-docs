# Enterprise Architecture Resource Library

ServiceNow is a powerful platform that can help you streamline your operations, automate your workflows, and improve your overall efficiency. But to get the most business value from
 our platform, you need to understand how the platform works and what it
xcan do for you.

[](https://www.servicenow.com/community/image/serverpage/image-id/250535i34D50D88890FAAC6/image-size/large?v=v2&px=999)

That's where our Enterprise Architecture 
Resource Library comes in. This library is a curated collection of 
articles, whitepapers, videos, and other resources that provide a wealth
 of information about ServiceNow. Whether you're just getting started 
with the platform or a seasoned pro, you'll find valuable insights and 
practical advice to help you achieve your goals.

[](https://www.servicenow.com/community/image/serverpage/image-id/335878i36433072CA3D92E2/image-size/large?v=v2&px=999)

[](https://www.servicenow.com/community/image/serverpage/image-id/250533iDB0C54EAC79F2D0A/image-size/large?v=v2&px=999)

The Enterprise Architecture Resource Library is
 intended to be simple to use and navigate. You can browse by topic or 
search for specific keywords for the needed information. We also want to
 keep the library updated regularly to ensure you have access to the 
latest insights and best practices.
