Question: What are the benefits and limitations of batch update sets?
Answer:
Benefits:

- Bulk migration
- Time-saving
- Improved organization
- Minimized dependency issues
- Promotes consistency

Limitations:

- No built-in dependency resolution
- Risk of conflicts
- Difficult to roll back
- Limited visibility
- No version control integration
