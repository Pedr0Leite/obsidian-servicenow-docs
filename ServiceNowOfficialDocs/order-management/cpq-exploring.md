# Exploring CPQ

CPQ is a next-generation configuration engine that redefines CPQ by acting as the “C” in [[configure-price-quote-landing|Configure, Price, Quote]]. It uses an attribute-based approach to simplify complex product logic, delivering dynamic [[blueprints|blueprints]], real-time user interfaces, and a powerful Commerce Logic Engine that generates accurate outputs like bills of materials.

## Overview of CPQ

With CPQ, organizations gain speed, flexibility, and precision in every sales and configuration experience.

## Key features

-   Attribute-Based Configurator: Goes beyond traditional add-ons to dynamically manage complex product logic.
-   Configuration Management: Handles product line items, BOMs, optional items, and configuration [[rules_101|rules]].
-   Pricing Engine: Supports standard and promotional pricing and enables holistic deal pricing across product bundles.
-   Approval Workflows: Offers automated or manual approvals to ensure compliance and manage risk.
-   Customer &amp; Fulfilment Details: Manages billing, shipping, staging, and delivery information.
-   Sales Enablement: Tracks deal stages, customer details, pricing, discounts, and terms, and generates customer-facing documents like formal quotes, terms &amp; conditions, and warranty documentation.

## Key capabilities

1.  [[fields|Fields]] and Rules: Capture attributes and enforce logic to ensure valid configurations.
2.  [[layouts|Layouts]]: Define the end-user experience with guided flows and visual cues.
3.  [[cpq-flightpath|Flightpath]]: Analyze rules engine behavior to diagnose performance and accuracy.
4.  [[cpq-using-cosmo-admin-assist|Cosmo Admin Assist]] &amp; SmartPredict: Provide AI-driven guidance to administrators for smarter configuration management.
5.  Visualization Integrations: Connect with partners like ThreeKit and kBridge to deliver real-time 2D/3D product renderings during configuration.

## Key benefits

-   Simplified Complexity: Attribute-based rules handle advanced product logic without creating endless bundles or SKU variations.
-   Faster Sales Cycles: Guided selling and dynamic blueprints help sales teams configure products and solutions more quickly.
-   Operational Efficiency: Centralized administration of rules, fields, and layouts reduces maintenance effort.
-   Integration Ready: Works seamlessly with Salesforce CPQ, eCommerce platforms, and visualization tools such as ThreeKit and kBridge.

## Key personas

Designed to support a broad set of users, each playing a critical role in configuration and sales processes:

-   Configuration Administrators: Define and manage blueprints, rules, and layouts to enforce consistency across the catalog.
-   IT Administrators: Oversee system controls, [[please_share_your_feedback_on_admin_assist_responses|user access]], and integrations to maintain secure and reliable deployments.
-   Sales Users: Use CPQ through Salesforce CPQ or eCommerce portals to configure solutions, generate quotes, and guide buyers confidently.
