# Using Now Assist Readiness Evaluation in Now Assist Center

Use [[now-assist-readiness-evaluation-landing-page|Now Assist Readiness Evaluation]] to help you prepare to launch generative AI and agentic AI for your organization.

The Now Assist Readiness Evaluation features in [[now-assist-center-landing-page|Now Assist Center]] help you find actionable items in your implementation preparation and provides direct links to improve those areas.

-   **[[now-assist-center-run-assessment-job|Run the AI readiness assessment job in Now Assist Center]]**  
Run the AI readiness assessment to analyze your instance and evaluate its readiness for AI adoption.
-   **[[view-ai-readiness-now-assist-center|View your AI readiness assessment in Now Assist Center]]**  
Review the readiness assessments that the Now Assist Readiness Evaluation has identified for your instance.
