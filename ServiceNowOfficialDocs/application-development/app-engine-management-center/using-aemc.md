# Using AEMC

AEMC enables you to [[manage-app-development|manage app development]] and deployments, all in one centralized location. Learn about how you can use AEMC to govern your organization's app development.

## Aims of AEMC

The [[app-engine-management-center|App Engine Management Center]] \(AEMC\) is designed to empower App Engine admins with all of the tools needed to make [[governing-app-development|governing app development]] easier. AEMC provides a unified experience for managing all stages of application development, from idea submission to [[get-started-deployment|deployment]]. Using AEMC, admins can manage requests for new apps and delegate development and [[collaboration-permissions|collaboration permissions]]. Admins can also oversee the entire deployment process with insights into individual deployment requests, test [[suites-module|suites]], and scheduled deployments. The following list outlines the tasks that the AEMC can help you to accomplish:

-   Mange intake requests submitted by developers for new apps.
-   Manage app requests submitted by no-code developers from within [[creator-studio-landing|Creator Studio]].
-   [[manage-collaboration-requests|Manage collaboration requests]] submitted by developers working on new apps.
-   [[manage-deployment-requests|Manage deployment requests]] for apps that are ready to be moved through the pipeline.
-   Schedule app deployments.
-   View insights about developers and custom apps within their ServiceNow environment.
