# Contract management view

Use the [[contract-mgmt-view-am|Contract management view]] in the [[using-ham-workspace|Hardware Asset Workspace]] to view and manage details of [[c_Contracts|contracts]] such as Contract number, Contract start and end dates, [[c_TermsAndConditions|terms and conditions]] statements.

The Contract Management view includes important actions for managing your contract, contract expenditure by type and vendor, and the list of expiring contracts.

\[Omitted image "contract-mgmt-view.png"\] Alt text: This view includes the contract overview details such as expiring contracts, contract expenditure by type and vendor.

To [[t_CreateAContract|create a contract]], select **New contract**. For more details, see [Create a contract](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/it-asset-management/contract-management/t_CreateAContract.md).
