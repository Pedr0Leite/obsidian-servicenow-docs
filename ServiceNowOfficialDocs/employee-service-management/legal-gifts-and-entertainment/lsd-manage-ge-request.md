# Managing gifts and entertainment disclosure requests

As a member of the Legal - Gifts Compliance group, review gifts and entertainment disclosure requests from employees and approve or reject them.

Use the [[legal-counsel-center-landing|Legal Counsel Center]] to manage gifts and entertainment disclosure requests.

-   **[Approve or reject a gifts and entertainment disclosure request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-gifts-and-entertainment/lsd-approve-reject-ge-request.md)**  
[[lsd-approve-reject-ge-request|Approve or reject a gifts and entertainment disclosure request]] assigned to you or a Legal - Gifts Compliance group of which you're a member.
-   **[Update a submitted gifts and entertainment disclosure request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-gifts-and-entertainment/lsd-update-ge-dis-record.md)**  
Update request details, add comments, or upload documents in a gifts and entertainment disclosure request you submitted while it is still in the New state. You can cancel a submitted request irrespective of its state.
